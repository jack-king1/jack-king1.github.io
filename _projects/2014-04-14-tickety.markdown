#### Tickety

<h2>What is Tickety?</h2>>
Tickety is an ecommerce website that solves the problem of printing display tickets manually by getting a third party to do the hard work (Tickety). Targeting butchers, bakeries and deli's. Users can create an account, keep track of tickets and in the future be able to order and buy custom tickets which will be sent straight to their door.
